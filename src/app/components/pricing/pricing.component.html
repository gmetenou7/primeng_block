Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a recurring fee or is the license perpetual?

PrimeBlocks license is perpetual.

Can I use PrimeBlocks license for commercial projects?

Yes, your license allows you to sell your project.

Can I create multiple projects for multiple clients?

You can create multiple projects for multiple clients and you need to document the license terms of PrimeBlocks in the project distributions.

Does PrimeBlocks include Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD files?

PrimeBlocks uses Figma as the design tool. You will be available to download the Figma files after your purchase.

What does lifetime support mean?

PrimeBlocks license is perpetual.

We’re a reseller, are we able to purchase a license on behalf of our client?

Yes, after the purchase, please contact us so we can transfer the license to your client.

Can I sell templates I build with PrimeBlocks?

No, you can’t re-distribute and use PrimeBlocks as stock, in a tool or template, or with source files. You can’t do this either on its own or bundle with other items.

Can I use PrimeBlocks in open-source projects?

Yes, you are allowed to create free and open-source projects. However, you are not allowed to create projects re-distributed on their own, bundled with other items, in a tool or template, and with source files.

What does "free updates" mean?

All updates including new designs and blocks will be totally free of charge for existing customers.

Is there a maintenance period for PrimeBlocks and the support?

No, updates are free of charge and support is unlimited and perpetual.

I’m a freelancer/agency. Can I use PrimeBlocks for a project that I’m doing for a client?

Yes. You’re essentially buying the license on your client’s behalf. You are allowed to charge your client for creating the project. Once you transfer the end product to the client (for example giving them source files for the product), be sure to link your client to our licenses and delete the item itself from your computer.

Can I create multiple projects with my PrimeBlocks license?

This license is a “multi-use” license and not a “single application” license, which means that you can use PrimeBlocks to create more than one unique project..

What JS framework does PrimeBlocks use?

PrimeBlocks uses Angular with the power of PrimeNG. It’ll available soon for other Prime libraries as well.

What browsers does PrimeBlocks support?

PrimeBlocks supports IE11, Firefox, Safari, Chrome and Edge.
